Hands-free Door Handle

COVID-19, as well as many other germs and viruses, can live on hard surfaces, which can transfer onto a person when touched. These touchless door handles allow doors to be opened without any skin contact, reducing the risk of contraction greatly.


Please ensure you are printing and delivering the requested type of door handle! The donor is also expcted to deliver the necessary hardware.

3D Files

Recommended Print Settings

Layer height: 0.15 mm

Infill: 30%

Material: Any

Supports: No

Additional Required Hardware

M4 Screws and Nuts necessary. Lengths and amounts vary with chosen design--see requested design for necessary hardware.
