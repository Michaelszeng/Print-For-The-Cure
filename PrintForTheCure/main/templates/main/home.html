The Movement

The Problem: PPE (Personal Protection Equipment) is critical to essential workers for safety, yet it is in critically low supply. Some PPE, such as face shields, face masks, mask comfort straps, and hand-less door openers can be easily produced at home.

How It Works in 5 Easy Steps:

  1. Doctors submit requests for PPE.
  2. Donors register through the website.
  3. Anyone can browse PPE requests on a map, and registered donors can view requests nearest to them.
  4. Donors can claim requests, after which they are connected with their requester.
  5. The donor then should fulfill the request for PPE.

By using this platform,

Doctors can benefit by receiving PPE.
Donors can help save the lives of their doctors and essential workers, and may receive monetary donations.
